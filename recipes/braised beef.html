braised beef

Description

This is a dish that is popular for Asian people, featuring tender beef and the sour taste from tomatoes

Ingredients

  • beef
  • tomatoes
  • potatoes
  • salt

Steps

  1. Cut beef into cubes
  2. Cut tomatoes and potatoes into pieces
  3. Add water to the pan, put all ingredients to the pan
  4. Add salt and boil 30 mins
